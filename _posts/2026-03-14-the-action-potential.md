> What separates the people who move from the people who wait, and why the answer is the most important skill to build.

Agency — the capacity to initiate action without waiting for perfect conditions or external permission — is the most irreplaceable human skill in an age of automation. The way to build it is to treat life as a series of small experiments, where failure is data, not defeat.
